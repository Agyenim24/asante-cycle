Programs

Sustainability projects

Beyond products, we fund environmental initiatives you can trace from contribution to outcome—reforestation, coastal recovery, and fair trade supply chains.

Tree planting program

For every purchase we contribute to reforestation across Indonesia. Native species are selected with local partners to restore watersheds and biodiversity.

15k+ trees since 2022

Coastal clean-up partnerships

We collaborate with NGOs and volunteers on regular beach and estuary clean-ups, with waste sorted for recycling where facilities exist.

5k+ kg waste collected

Fair trade supplier network

Small-scale farmers receive fair pricing and support for eco-friendly production—reducing chemical inputs and building resilient local economies.

100+ farmers · Southeast Asia

How projects are funded

A dedicated share of revenue from qualifying product lines goes to these programs. We review partner performance annually and adjust funding based on measurable results and community feedback.

For more detail on outcomes, visit our Impact page or read stories on the Blog.

Packaging innovation

Recycled and compostable materials for shipping—tested for durability so your orders arrive safely with less waste.

Community workshops

On-the-ground education on composting, low-waste cooking, and plastic alternatives in partner regions.
