Impact

Our impact in numbers

We track plastic avoided, trees planted, waste diverted from coastlines, and carbon-neutral shipping—so you can see progress year over year.

10k+ Plastic reduced (kg)

How we measure

We work with third-party partners where possible to validate waste and emissions data. Plastic figures reflect packaging and logistics changes versus a baseline year. Tree planting is tracked through our reforestation partners in Indonesia.

Coastal clean-up weights include sorted recyclables and material sent for responsible disposal. We publish an annual impact summary and refine our methodology as standards evolve.
